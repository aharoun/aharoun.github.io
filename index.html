About

I'm a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Pennsylvania.

My research interest lies in macroeconomics, with a particular interest in firm dynamics, economic growth and innovation. Before starting my PhD, I worked on topics related to monetary policy, macroeconomic forecasting and policy analysis.
